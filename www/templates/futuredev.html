Future Development

The list below outlines some of the plans I have to further develop this application to maximize its utility. If you have suggestions, feel free to email me at : shelleydaviswise@gmail.com

  • -User name and password authorization - originally a working part of the application, I had to remove it for the time being.
  • -The ability to edit parameters associated with each child and keep a record of the changes.
  • -The ability to delete children from the application.
  • -The ability to associate an image with each child.
  • - A more dynamic calculation of allowance increment. As it stands, each child's allowance is calculated based on a daily rate multiplied by the static number of days since being added to the app. I would like the increment to act independently of a static point. Doing so would allow for easier transition to edited parameters.
